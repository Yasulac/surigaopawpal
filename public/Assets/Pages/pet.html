How it works?

The adoption process for Surigao PawPal designed to ensure a smooth, safe, and responsible
experience for both adopters and pets.

01.

Browse pet profiles

Explore the available pets listed on
the platform, filtering by various criteria such as breed, age, size, and temperament.

02.

Select a Pet

Finds a you interested in, you can click on the pet's profile to view more details.

03.

Submit an Adoption Application

Fill out an adoption application form on the pet’s profile page

04.

Application Review

You may contact the applicant for more details, arrange a phone or in-person interview, or ask additional questions to ensure the adopter is a good fit
